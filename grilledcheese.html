HOW TO GRILL A CHEESE!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, sourdough, or your favorite)
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • 1-2 tablespoons butter (softened)

Steps

  1. Butter the bread:

    Spread butter evenly on one side of each slice of bread.

  2. Assemble the sandwich:

    Place one slice of bread butter-side down in a skillet. Add the cheese slices on top, then cover with the second slice of bread (butter-side up).

  3. Cook the first side:

    Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium heat. Cook the sandwich for about 2-3 minutes, or until the bottom is golden brown.

  4. Flip and cook the other side:

    Carefully flip the sandwich with a spatula. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, until the second side is golden and the cheese is melted.

  5. Serve:

    Remove from heat, let it cool for a minute, then slice in half and enjoy! ðŸ¥ª
